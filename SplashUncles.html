Utah Jazz: Splash Uncles!

Splash Uncles was something I worked on with 3 other students from Neumont for the Utah Jazz. Other than the basic structure code and the player model, all of it was created by the 4 of us in a limited time frame. My focus was on the coding end, hooking things up to the structure and coding new systems.

My biggest accomplishments from this project I would say are:

  • A dynamic world curving shader to give the world more of a sense of traveling
  • Asyncronous loading of new stages, allowing the game to continue running while new assets are loading and pooling
  • Customizable enviroment spawning system, allowing for control over the procedural spawning of the visual accents and walls
  • Hooking up to a backend database to store tracking metrics as well as global High Scores
