Boney Hawk's Wild Ride

Dispite having only about 2 weeks of work put into it, Boney Hawk still remains one of my personal favorite projects.

It is fairly simple, a distance game where you play as a skeleton snowboarding your own coffin down a mountain.

I made it fairly early into my education and I have plans on making a sequel at some point due to my love for the concept.
